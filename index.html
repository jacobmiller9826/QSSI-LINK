Why Link?

Link structures adversary movement through predictable checkpoints that provide robust telemetry and automated hardening. This allows clean exits and faster decision-making when things go wrong.

  • Sequential barriers yield predictable traversal.
  • Chained observability correlates events quickly.
  • Adaptive hardening tightens adjacent nodes on alert.
  • Export & Simulate to validate designs.
